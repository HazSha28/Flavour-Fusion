Bites,Brunch & Bowls

🥗1. HIGH PROTEIN BOWL

    Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken, quinoa, chickpeas
  • Spinach, cherry tomatoes, avocado
  • Greek yogurt dressing

    Steps:

  1. Cook quinoa and season grilled chicken.
  2. Layer bowl with greens, protein, and veggies.
  3. Top with yogurt dressing and serve.
🍌2. PEACH BANANA SMOOTHIE BOWL
     

    Ingredients:

     
  • 1 ripe banana (preferably frozen)
    •  
  • 1 cup frozen or fresh peach slices
    •  
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
    •  
  • ½ cup milk (dairy or plant-based like almond milk)
    •  
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
    •  
  • Toppings: banana slices, peach slices, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, nuts
     

    Steps:

     
  1. Blend the banana, peach slices, Greek yogurt, milk, and honey until smooth and thick.
    2.  
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with your choice of toppings like fruits, granola, and seeds.
🍳3. TURKISH POACHED EGGS

    Ingredients:

  • Eggs, yogurt, garlic, paprika
  • Butter, dill, salt, vinegar

    Steps:

  1. Poach eggs in vinegar water.
  2. Mix yogurt with garlic and salt, spread on plate.
  3. Top with poached eggs and paprika butter drizzle.
🍳4. MINI FRITTATA

    Ingredients:

  • Eggs, spinach, cheese, bell peppers
  • Onions, salt, pepper, olive oil

    Steps:

  1. Beat eggs with seasoning and chopped veggies.
  2. Pour into muffin tray and bake at 180°C for 20 mins.
🍳5. SHAKSHUKA WITH FETA

    Ingredients:

  • Eggs, tomatoes, onion, bell pepper
  • Garlic, paprika, feta cheese, olive oil

    Steps:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and bell peppers.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer till thick.
  3. Crack eggs into the mix, top with feta, cook till set.
🍚6. GARLIC RICE WITH WHITE SAUCE CHICKEN
       

      Ingredients:

       
    • Cooked basmati rice, chopped garlic, butter, salt, pepper
      •  
    • Boneless chicken, milk, all-purpose flour, butter, minced garlic
      •  
    • Black pepper, cheese (optional), mixed herbs or oregano
       

      Steps:

       
    1. Sauté chopped garlic in butter, add rice, season with salt and pepper.
      2.  
    2. Cook chicken with garlic, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
      3.  
    3. Make white sauce by mixing butter and flour, then slowly whisking in milk.
      4.  
    4. Add cooked chicken and seasonings to the sauce. Simmer for a few minutes.
      5.  
    5. Serve creamy white sauce chicken over garlic rice.
🥬7. COLESLAW

    Ingredients:

  • Cabbage, carrots, mayonnaise, vinegar
  • Sugar, salt, pepper

    Steps:

  1. Shred cabbage and carrots finely.
  2. Mix with mayo, vinegar, sugar, salt & pepper.
  3. Chill before serving.
🍓8. FRUIT SALAD

    Ingredients:

  • Mixed fruits: apple, banana, berries, orange
  • Honey, lemon juice, mint

    Steps:

  1. Chop all fruits evenly.
  2. Drizzle honey and lemon juice.
  3. Mix gently and garnish with mint.
